Chicken Spaghetti Bake

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350F (175C)
  2. Mix the dry ingredients in a large bowl. Bring the water to a boil
  3. Add vinegar and egg whites to the dry ingredients, and combine well. Add boiling water, while beating with a hand mixer for about 30 second. Don't over mix the dough, the consistency should resemble Play-Doh.
  4. Moisten hands with a little olive oil and shape dough into 6 separate rolls. Place on a greased baking sheet. Top with optional sesame seeds.

Ingredients

  • Ground psyllium husk powder
    80 ml
  • Almond flour
    300 ml
  • Baking powder
    2 tsp
  • Sea salt
    1 tsp
